About me

Hello!

My name is Paulina Dybała – but you can know me as lazulillustrates.

I’m a children’s book illustrator and graphic designer based in Poland. I graduated from Art High School in Kraków, and currently living here with my partner and companion, little black cat. I’ve always been a person full of imagination and ideas, I used to create my own stories and character since I was a kid and this passion continued to grow with me till this day. I have commercial experience with creating stickers, washitapes and other accessories, recently I also pursued working on illustrations for children’s poetry book – Ważności – which was published in May 2021. Since then I’m working on another projects, hoping one day I’ll be able to write and illustrate my own book.

For work and publishing inquires – please reach out to me via email: lazulillustrates@gmail.com
